Frequently Asked Questions

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process includes document verification, background checks, and skill assessment. Workers must provide valid ID proofs, address verification, and undergo a thorough screening process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. Payments are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

How do I report an issue?

You can report issues through our support portal, contact form, or by calling our 24/7 support line. Our team will respond within 2 hours.

What happens if a worker doesn't show up?

We have a strict attendance policy. If a worker doesn't show up, we'll immediately provide a replacement worker at no additional cost.
